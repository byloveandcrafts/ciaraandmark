How It Began

Our Love Story

“
2017

Our love story began when we crossed paths at Don Honorio Ventura Technological State University during our college days. What started as a simple friendship slowly turned into something beautiful and unexpected — I was an Education student, while he was taking up Civil Engineering, yet despite our different paths, fate brought our hearts together.

2018

From sharing laughs, late-night conversations, and supporting each other through the struggles of college life, we found comfort and happiness in one another. Little by little, our friendship blossomed into love, and we officially became a couple.

Through the years, we grew together, faced challenges hand in hand, and celebrated every achievement as a team. He became my safe place, my greatest supporter, and my best friend. No matter how much time passed, our love only became stronger and deeper.

”

Now, after years of love, patience, and countless memories together,
we are finally preparing to say "I do" — and begin forever with each other.
